Work Experience

Freelance Designer
Jun 2015 - Current

As a freelance designer, I’ve consistently challenged myself to produce stronger and more memorable visuals with every assignment. Through a refined process, I'm able to tackle a vast diversity of projects with mechanical efficiency. By taking clients' business ideas and converting them into professionally styled and functioning online marketplaces, I've gained a thorough understanding of branding, web design, print production and social media content creation. Above all, the most rewarding is working with clients every step of the way to create an image that proudly reflects their product and goals as a business.

It starts with a name or a logo. I sketch some rough ideas in a notebook to make sure I'm on the right track. With a concept approved, I use Adobe Illustrator to create the first digital drafts and experiment with typefaces and colors for branding guidelines. Often times I'll create a sub set of logos to be used in different areas of marketing with height or width restrictions.

Next is creating a web presense that supports all feature requirements. A mostly static page can be built purely with HTML, CSS and PHP. For more complex features (i.e. accepting payments) I'll use existing online platforms and APIs. Email accounts are set up and basic SEO/SEM marketing is in place.

When the online section is running smoothing, it's time to get traffic. Social media accounts are set up with relevant content and print marketing materials are discussed and produced (i.e. brochures, business cards, apparel, etc.)

I work directly with the printers and take great care in file preparation by checking colors, bleeds, margins, folds, file types, dimensions, and scaling. If the client has a physical product, I produce photo realistic mock-ups to show exactly how their labels and packaging will arrive at their door.
